For all grade levels, doing an ESPN Goodbye from any location offers the added benefit of staggering the relocation of your students to any given space, whether it is the hallway outside your classroom, a bus lot, a car lot, or an after-school care room or area.

The main benefit of the ESPN Goodbye is so very important: to let each and every student know that, even though you are not required to do it, you are taking the time to say “goodbye” to them because you care about them and like them enough to do it. This consistent routine will improve the lives of your students, especially those who may not be experiencing consistent routines and expressions of care at home.

* * * * * * * * * * *

By now you should be noticing that these procedures complement and enhance each other: procedures that build relationships also share control, and procedures that share control help to build relationships. When used together, these procedures turn classrooms into control-sharing and relationship-building machines. If you set the machine into motion in your first three weeks, it will create and perpetuate a classroom environment that is safe, calm, and kind—a place where students can work, struggle, grow, and learn.
