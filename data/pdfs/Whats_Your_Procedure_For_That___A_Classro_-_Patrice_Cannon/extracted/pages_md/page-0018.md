# The Blue Teacher
- This teacher is student-centered.
- Naturally caters to the child's social/emotional well-being.
- Involves every student.
- Works diligently to ensure all students are safe in class.
- Nurturing.
- Takes time to explore the root cause issues.

# The Blue Student
- Often needs reassurance.
- Relational.
- Likes to keep the peace.
- Sensitive.
- Wants to make others happy.
- Cares about how others feel.
- Prefers opportunities to socialize.
- Needs physical touch, such as a hug or pat on the back.

## Dear Blue Teachers, Your Students Need You to Know...

### Orange Student
“I like that you try to understand my behavior.”  
“If you do not give me structure, then I will take advantage of the freedom.”  
“I like that you are understanding about late work, because more than likely, I procrastinated.”  

### Gold Student
“It does not matter how we feel about doing it. We just need to get it done.”  
“I like that you appreciate how organized I am.”  
“Please do not tell me to stop taking things so seriously. It is who I am.”  

### Blue Student
“I like that you stand outside the door in the morning, giving us compliments and praise.”  
“I like the opportunities you give us to work in groups.”  
“I like that you spend one-on-one time with me and give me feedback.”  

### Green Student
“I need time to work alone.”  
“I know you think everyone is a winner, but did I get the highest score?”  
“The people in my group do not know as much as I do about this subject.”
