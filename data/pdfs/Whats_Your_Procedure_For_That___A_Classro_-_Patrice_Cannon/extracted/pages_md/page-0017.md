## Blue Personality

### BLUE TEAM

#### Traits & Benefits
- Optimistic
- Accepting
- Supportive
- Caretaker
- Enthusiastic
- Passionate
- Empathetic
- Peacemaker
- Cooperative
- Spiritual
- People-oriented

#### Stressors
- Conflict
- Isolation
- Rejection
- Negativity
- Being “used”
- Apathy
- Too much criticism
- Lack of acknowledgement or appreciation
- Not being genuine
- Not sharing
- Being embarrassed

## Perceptions

### See themselves as:
- Caring → Too emotional
- Trusting → Naïve
- Giving benefit of the doubt → Over-committed
- Tirelessly working for a cause → Co-dependent
- Supportive → Smothering
- Genuinely interested in others → Nosy
