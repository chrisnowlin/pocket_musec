## A BLUEPRINT FOR FULL IMPLEMENTATION

**Step 1: Decide if you can do this.**

While it’s not as daunting as being a coach or a director, attending events still takes up some of your personal time. Decide if you’re willing to do it, and if so, if you can put on a happy face while doing it. Students will immediately recognize if you’re not enjoying yourself, and just showing up to “check the box” won’t strengthen your classroom rapport. If you’re going to do it, do it right.
