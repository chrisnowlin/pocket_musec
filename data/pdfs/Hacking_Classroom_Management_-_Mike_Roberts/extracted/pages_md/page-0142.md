# HACKING EDUCATION
## 10 Quick Fixes For Every School
By Mark Barnes (@markbarnes19) & Jennifer Gonzalez (@cultofpedagogy)

In the bestselling *Hacking Education*, Mark Barnes and Jennifer Gonzalez employ decades of teaching experience and hundreds of discussions with education thought leaders to show you how to find and hone the quick fixes that every school and classroom need. Using a Hacker’s mentality, they provide **one Aha moment after another** with *10 Quick Fixes For Every School*—solutions to everyday problems and teaching methods that any teacher or administrator can implement immediately.
