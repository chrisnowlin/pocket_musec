## History of Mexico <1930

| | |
| --- | --- |
| **Okie Migration** | Desperate, poor, farmers arrive in masses—up to 7,000 a month. There are not enough jobs and they are often turned away at the border. They are disparagingly called “Okies.” |
| **Labor Unrest** | Farm workers form unions and strike in response to poor treatment by farm owners. |
