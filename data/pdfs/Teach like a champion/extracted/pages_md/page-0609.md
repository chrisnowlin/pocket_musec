### Developmental Writing Scope and Sequence

| Progression | Core Developmental Writing Prompts (examples use “Esperanza opened her eyes …”) |
|-------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Grade 5     | • **Combine sentences**: Esperanza opened her eyes. She had been sleeping. She dreamed that her father was with her. |
| Grade 5     | • **Expand with because, but, so**: “Esperanza opened her eyes so she saw that it had not been a dream.” |
| Grade 5     | • **Expand with after, during, before**: “Esperanza opened her eyes during a dream in which her father was still present.” |
| Grade 5     | • **Expansion (E)**: Add details explaining three to five of the Five Ws: “Esperanza opened her eyes when she woke up, feeling anxious, in her own bed.” |
| Grade 5     | • **Expand with appositives**: “Esperanza, a young girl whose father had just been killed, opened her eyes.” |
