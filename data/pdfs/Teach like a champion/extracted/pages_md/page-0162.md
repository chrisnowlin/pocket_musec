## Poetic and Literary Terms

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Reliability** | The degree to which a person's narration or memory is trustworthy or accurate |

## Words to Describe Family and Heritage

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Ancestry** | The line of people in a family's past |
| **Genealogy** | An account of a person or family's descent from past generations |
| **Heredity** | The passing of personal characteristics from one family's generation to another; we say that a trait that is passed (e.g., brown eyes) is **inherited** or **hereditary** |
