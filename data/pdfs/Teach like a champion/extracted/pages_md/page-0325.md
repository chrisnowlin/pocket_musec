For students to encode concepts in their long-term memory, they need to consolidate knowledge from multiple sources. *Stretch It* questions can help students practice this consolidation.  

Teacher: What might Bradbury be alluding to in this image?  
Student: Maybe a bomb that went off, destroying the whole town.  
Teacher: What do we know was happening in America at the time Bradbury was writing?  
Student: The United States ended World War II by dropping nuclear bombs on Hiroshima and Nagasaki.  
Teacher: So what might Bradbury be alluding to specifically?  
Student: This image might be an allusion to nuclear destruction, like what happened in Japan in 1945.
