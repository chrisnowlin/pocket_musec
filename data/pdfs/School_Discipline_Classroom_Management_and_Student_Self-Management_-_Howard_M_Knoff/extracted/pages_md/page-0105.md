4. Q&A: Additional clarifying questions, identification of relevant unknown information or hypotheses to explain the concerns from the SPRINT team (10 minutes).

5a. Too many relevant unknowns: Assign individuals to collect the information and determine when to reconvene at a future SPRINT meeting (3 minutes).
5b. Sufficient information to proceed: Assignment of a master consultant to work with the teacher in the classroom on functional assessment to interventions.

- Special education referrals or placements for students
- Number of students moving to less restrictive settings or programs, or who no longer need services through an academic or behavioral intervention plan
