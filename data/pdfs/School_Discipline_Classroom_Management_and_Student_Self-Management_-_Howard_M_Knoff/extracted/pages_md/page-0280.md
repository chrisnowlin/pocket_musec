## PBSS Implementation Case Study: NOVA Academy Alternative School, St. Bernard Parish, Louisiana

Project ACHIEVE began its relationship with the New Opportunities and Values for Achievement (NOVA) Academy in April 2002 when three of its support staff attended a four-day national Project ACHIEVE Training of Trainers sponsored by the U.S. Department of Health & Human Service’s Center for Substance Abuse Prevention. NOVA Academy was the middle and high school level alternative school for the St. Bernard Parish School District, a largely poor and rural school system to the east of New Orleans, directly on the Gulf of Mexico near the Mississippi River. Project ACHIEVE training with the NOVA staff began on-site in July 2002 and continued through August 2009.
