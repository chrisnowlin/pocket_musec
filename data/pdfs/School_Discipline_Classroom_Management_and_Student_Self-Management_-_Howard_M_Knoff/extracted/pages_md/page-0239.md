## PBSS Implementation Case Study: Jesse Keen Elementary School, Lakeland, Florida

Project ACHIEVE was conceived and first implemented at Jesse Keen Elementary School during the 1990 to 1991 school year as part of a partnership with the Polk County School District. Jesse Keen was a full-service school, a Chapter I schoolwide school, and it established and staffed a parent drop-in center that provided parent training and outreach services during the 1990s. Supported over 10 years through six U.S. Department of Education, Office of Special Education Program grants, Jesse Keen’s staff received training in every component of Project ACHIEVE largely over a five-year period of time.
