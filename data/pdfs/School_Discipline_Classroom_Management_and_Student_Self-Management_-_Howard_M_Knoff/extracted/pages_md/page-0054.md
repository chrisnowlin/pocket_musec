## PBSS Implementation Case Study: McDonald Elementary School, Seffner, Florida

McDonald Elementary School is in the Hillsborough County School District of Tampa, Florida, which is the tenth largest school district in the country. In 1998, we gave the School Leadership Team (SLT) and the entire school staff at McDonald an overview of the three-year Positive Behavioral Support System (PBSS) blueprint as part of our entry process. As we continued to work with the SLT and staff, we proceeded through a multifaceted organizational and motivational analysis of the readiness of the school for PBSS implementation. One aspect of the motivational analysis involved a formal vote by the staff to reflect their commitment to the three (or more) year process. A typical benchmark for readiness is a minimum 80% vote in favor.
