# Musical Maze

Amelia was sitting in her backyard listening to music and tending to her garden. She enjoyed all of the songs that she heard, but she decided to pick a flower for every song that she thought was especially beautiful. Follow the path marked by whole notes to get from start to finish. When you are done, count the number of whole notes to find how many flowers Amelia picked.
