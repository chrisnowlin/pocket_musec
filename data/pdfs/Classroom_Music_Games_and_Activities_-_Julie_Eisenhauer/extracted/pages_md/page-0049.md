# Music Facts Finger Fun

**Directions:**  
1. Spin the spinner and move Finger Fun the number of times indicated.  
2. Open the flap matching the letter indicated on the spinner.  
3. Read the question out loud, and then decide the answer.  
4. Open the flap to look at the correct answer.  

*Note: Score one point for each correct answer.*  

## Instrument Identity  

- **A**: Cello  
  - I have 4 strings and I am larger than both the Violin and the Viola.  
- **A**: Trombone  
  - I use a U-shaped slide to change pitches.  
- **B**: Tuba  
  - I am the largest and lowest sounding instrument in the Brass family.  
- **B**: String, Woodwind, Brass, Percussion  
  - Name the four instrument families.  
- **C**: Percussion  
  - A Tambourine belongs to what instrument family?  
- **C**: Six  
  - How many strings does a Guitar have?  
- **D**: True  
  - True or False: An Oboe is a double-reed instrument.  
- **D**: I am made of brass, but I use a woodwind reed to produce sound.
