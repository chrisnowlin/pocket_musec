See if you can catch and color the instruments based on the family to which they belong.  
The instrument families are listed below, along with instructions for how to color each.  

- Color the Brass instruments blue.  
- Color the Percussion instruments red.  
- Color the String instruments yellow.  
- Color the Woodwind instruments green.
